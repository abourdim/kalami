Quick Start — 3 Steps
١

Open in Chrome or Edge

Open kalami-v2.1.html directly in Google Chrome or Microsoft Edge. Safari and Firefox are not supported — they lack the Web Speech Recognition API.

٢

Allow Microphone Access

When prompted, click Allow to grant microphone permission. This is required for Arabic speech recognition. Kalami never records or stores your voice.

٣

Click the Microphone & Speak Arabic

Click the 🎙️ button or press Space. Speak naturally in Arabic. Your words appear as text, then translate to French 🇫🇷 and English 🇬🇧 automatically.

💡 Best results: Speak at a natural pace in a quiet environment. Use the dialect selector to match your Arabic dialect (MSA, Egyptian, Maghrebi, Levantine, or Iraqi) for better recognition accuracy.

Translation Modes
Mode How to Use Best For Translation Lag
✋ Manual Click mic → speak → click again to stop Single sentences, quiet environment Best quality
🔄 Continuous Toggle switch ON → mic stays open forever Live conversations, hands-free Per pause
⚡ Near-Live Enable Near-Live toggle → translates each phrase chunk Live interpretation, lectures ~2–3 seconds
⚡ Near-Live tip: Combine Near-Live with a single speak language (FR or EN, not Both) for the fastest response. The audio queue ensures voice output never overlaps.

All Features
🏷️

Dialect Selector

Choose MSA/Gulf, Egyptian, Maghrebi, Levantine, or Iraqi dialect. Matching your dialect significantly improves recognition accuracy.

✏️

Edit Transcript

Click directly on the Arabic transcript box to correct any recognition errors before translating. Fully editable, RTL-aware.

⚡

Translation Memory

Phrases you've already translated are cached in-session. Repeating the same phrase gives an instant result with a ⚡ badge.

↩️

Back-Translation Verify

Enable "↩ Verify" to translate FR/EN back to Arabic under each entry. Spot-check translation accuracy instantly.

🔊

Voice Selector

Your browser may have multiple FR and EN voices installed. Pick the one you prefer from the dropdown in each language panel.

🎚️

Speech Rate

Slow down to 0.5× for learners or speed up to 2× for native listeners. Controlled independently per language.

⭐

Star Entries

Click the ☆ star on any translation card to bookmark it. Starred entries are highlighted in gold and persist through searches.

🔍

Search History

Type in the search box above each panel to filter translations by keyword. Useful for long sessions.

🖥️

Fullscreen Mode

Click ⛶ on any panel to expand it to fullscreen — ideal for presentations or showing translations to a group.

📑

Export PDF

Exports a bilingual PDF with Arabic, French, and English for each entry. Opens the print dialog — save as PDF.

🔡

Font Size Slider

Adjust text size across all panels with the Aa slider in the controls bar. Great for accessibility or presentations.

⌨️

Arabic Keyboard

Full on-screen Arabic keyboard with all 28 letters, numerals ١-٩, punctuation, and a direct translate button.

❖ ❖ ❖
⌨️
Arabic Virtual Keyboard
١

Open the Keyboard

Click the ⌨️ Arabic Keyboard button in the input panel, or press K on your keyboard.

٢

Type Arabic Letters

Click any letter to insert it at the cursor position in the transcript box. Use ⌫ Del to delete the last character, ⌦ Word to delete the last word, and مسافة for a space.

٣

Translate Directly

Click the golden ⚡ Translate key on the keyboard row — no need to reach for the button above.

💡 Tip: The keyboard is perfect for correcting speech recognition errors. Just click the wrong letter, use backspace, and retype it. Then hit ⚡ Translate right from the keyboard.
